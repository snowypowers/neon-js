createScript()¶

A wrapper method around ScripBuilder for creating a VM script.

Arguments:
  • props (Object) – Properties passed in as an object.
  • props.scriptHash (string) – The contract scriptHash.
  • props.operation (string) – The method name to call.
  • props.args (Array) – The arguments of the method to pass in.
  • props.useTailCall (boolean) – To use Tail Call.
